3 years later, still chasing the dream...

Quick story time:

When Dinos and Takis were 19, they had an idea that sounded impossible: move to San Francisco. Not for vacation, but to be at the heart of technology. The place where everything starts.

No access, no network, no money, and no one who knew what YC or an accelerator even was. We had none of that. But we did have an obsession that would not go away.

Three years later, after failures, mistakes, unanswered emails, closed doors, and a few that finally opened, our team is now permanently in Silicon Valley.

But the longer we live here, the more one thing bothers us: it should not be this hard for a young person from Greece to get close to this world.
